About Me

Hi, my name is Linda and I am a professional real estate agent. With an intimate knowledge of all the neighborhoods of the nation's capital, I have a finger on the pulse of the Washington, DC metro area.

My proven skills and uniquely personal approach make me the best choice for listing or buying. I provide an unparalleled, high level of customer service and trusted advice to my clients and friends. A big bonus was that I had more flexibility to be more available for and involved with my family that I created through getting married in DC in 2010.

Born in Denver, Colorado, I combine decades of international sales experience and strong negotiating skills to distinguish myself among a handful of top-producing multilingual real estate agents in the Washington metro area. I speak English, Spanish and Portuguese.
